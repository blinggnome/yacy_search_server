Dead Domains

During crawling, YaCy can detect domains that now redirect to domain-sale or parked-domain landing pages. This tool controls whether those detected dead domains are only reported in the crawler monitor or are automatically cleaned up.

When automatic cleanup is enabled, detected domains are added to url.domain_for_sale.black with root and subdomain rules, and indexed URLs for that domain and discovered subdomains are removed.
